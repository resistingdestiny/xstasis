## The Beginning

I grew up in the countryside where my nearest shop was a 45 minute walk away. It was only in the first year of university I developed an interest in nightlife, where suddenly it was possible to go somewhere at midnight and find other people there!

My interest only continued to grow while living in Beijing for my second year of university, where the comparatively empty and, with the benefit of hindsight, sleepy student nightclubs of Oxford gave way to spectacles of lights, sounds, smoke and lasers. I had a brief foray into the curious world of Chinese night club promotion, where my mission was to bring in foreigners (who would be happy to dance) to make the club more attractive to the locals who primarily wanted to sit around tables and drink Bai Jiu.

## The Challenge

My mission to visit the top 100 clubs in the world as defined by DJ Magazine has been fraught with challenges. Firstly, it has not been easy to find the time to make it out clubbing while running an insurance software company which has required me to travel all over the world across our hubs in America, Bermuda and the United Kingdom. My own motivation for following through on the challenge has been full of ups and downs.

For instance, despite going to Prague more than a dozen times across 2023 and 2024, I never set foot in Duplex, the only club on the list. Similarly, when going for brief periods of time, frequently the twin barriers of time, interest and me even remembering there was a club in the city presented problems.

I was fortunate however to have built my business with my best friend, who, though I will never admit it to his face, was probably the only reason I got into exclusive Berlin establishment [[Berghain]].

## The Reality

The other element of the top 100 clubs list is that it is in constant flux. While nightlife comes under strain in some areas from the pressures of development and gentrification, new hubs of nightlife emerge elsewhere.

The mission is very focused on clubs, but I have to admit some of my favourite memories lie well away from the bright lights of the most well known venues. Some undoubtedly have their place reinforced by an army of savvy club PRs who encourage participation in the voting process. The mission is as much about straying off the beaten track, with the top 100 acting as a guidepoint to places I might not otherwise have looked in search of music and vibes.

## The Journey Continues

This archive is the documentation of that journey. The wins, the losses, the incredible nights and the ones where sleep or circumstance won out. Every club has a story, every night a memory. Some of the best moments happened in places that will never make the top 100. Some of the most hyped venues left me cold.

That's the beauty of it. The list is a guide, not a gospel. The mission is discovery, not completion.

The dancefloor awaits.
