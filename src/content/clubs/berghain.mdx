## Overview

Berghain is not just a club. It's a cultural institution. Located in a former power plant in East Berlin, this brutalist monument to techno has become the most famous club in the world.

## The Space

The main floor is a cathedral of sound. The ceiling soars overhead, the concrete walls absorb and reflect the music in perfect balance. The sound system is legendary, designed by the club specifically for the space.

## The Music

Primarily techno and minimal, though the Panorama Bar upstairs leans more house. Resident DJs include some of the biggest names in electronic music.

## Getting In

The door policy is strict and famously unpredictable. Dress in black, be respectful, don't speak English loudly in line. Most importantly, don't take photos inside.

## Tips

- Go late (after 2 AM) or very early (Sunday afternoon)
- Bring cash for the coat check and drinks
- Be prepared to stay for 12+ hours
- The smoking area offers a moment of respite
