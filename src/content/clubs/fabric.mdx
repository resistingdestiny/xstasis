## Overview

Fabric is London's legendary superclub, known for its three rooms of cutting-edge electronic music and one of the best sound systems in the world. Located in Farringdon, it's been a pillar of the city's nightlife since 1999.

## The Rooms

**Room 1** is the main space, featuring the famous bodysonic dancefloor where bass frequencies vibrate through the floor itself. **Room 2** leans more experimental and bass-heavy. **Room 3** is intimate and often features house and techno.

## The Sound

Each room has a custom sound system that's been fine-tuned for years. The bass in Room 1 is a physical experience that has to be felt to be believed.

## Programming

Fabric runs three distinct club nights:
- **fabric** (Fridays) - house and techno
- **FABRICLIVE** (Saturdays) - drum and bass, dubstep, grime
- Special events and label showcases

## Survival Guide

- Buy tickets in advance, especially for big names
- Arrive before midnight to avoid the queue
- The smoking area is on the roof
- Stay hydrated, the dancefloor gets intense
