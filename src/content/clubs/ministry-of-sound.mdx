## Overview

Ministry of Sound is a legendary London superclub that has been a cornerstone of UK dance music since 1991. Located in Elephant and Castle, it's one of the most recognizable club brands in the world.

## The Space

The club features multiple rooms, with The Box as the main room featuring a world-class sound system designed specifically for the space. The venue has been carefully acoustically treated to deliver pristine sound quality.

## The Sound

Ministry is known for its diverse programming spanning house, techno, trance, and everything in between. Friday nights often lean more commercial, while Saturdays bring in underground talent.

## Legacy

Beyond the club itself, Ministry of Sound became a global brand with its compilation albums defining an era of dance music. The iconic logo is recognized worldwide.

## Tips

- Book tickets in advance for big names
- The Box is where the magic happens
- Nearby parking and transport links make it accessible
- Can get very packed on peak nights
